Festival Info

Enter the World of Synthwave

For newcomers, Synth wave is a style of music that fits into the neon-noir style that came out of the 1980’s and has seen a revival as of late through various forms of media. The Outrunners music festival celebrates this culture, and showcases the world’s leading Dj’s and Musicians that have devoted their efforts to forwarding the genre with cutting, synthesizer fueled tracks. Expect to be transported back to a time when neon lights soaked the streets, where Ray Bans and Walkmans were as ubiquitous as iPods, and where the music was played loud.
